kevin rabinovich

Hi, I’m Kevin. I really love the intersections of ideas, youth, technology, design, and education.

I’m the founder (and now curator) of TEDxYouth@Columbia, South Carolina’s by-youth, for-youth organization that unites high school students around the power of ideas.

Since last year, I’ve been the founding co-president & creative director at Clemson UNICEF.

In high school, I organized CodeDay Columbia, a 24-hour hackathon for high school and college students, and was the regional manager for 3 seasons.

I’ve been an intern for 52inc, truematter, and IT-oLogy — all in Columbia, SC. I currently serve as an advisor to Student Space.

I have also produced videos for TEDxYouth@Columbia, as well as some satirical ones just for fun on my YouTube channel.

I invite you to have a look at my design portfolio or some of my writing. You can also find me on Designer News, GitHub, & Twitter.

My résumé is available upon request.
